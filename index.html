CROATIA

Southern Croatia.

Dubrovnik,

CROATIA WAS A STUNNING SURPRISE MY

PARENTS PLANNED. EVERY TIME I MISS

SUMMER I REMEMBER CROATIA'S

STUNNINGVIEWS, BEAUTIFUL WEATHER,

KIND PEOPLE AND THE OVERALL DREAM

EXPEIRENCE I HAD THERE DURING MY

VISIT. I COMPARE EVERY TRIP I TAKE

AFTER THAT ONE TO CROATIA.

A COUNTRY THAT WITHSTOOD WARS AND

DESTRUCTION, IT'S PEOPOLE HAVE DONE

AN AMAZING JOB AT RESTORING AND

PERSERVING IT'S HISOTORY AND BEAUTY.

ALONG WITH IT'S OBVIOUS VISUAL

AESTHETIC, I LEARNED SO MUCH

ABOUT IT'S HISTORIC BEAUTY, STORY AND

PEOPLE. I CAME TO APPRECIATE AND LOVE

IT FOR MUCH MORE THAN ITS VIEWS.
